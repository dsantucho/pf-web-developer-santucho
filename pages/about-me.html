About me & work

I have over 10 years of experience in QA and am currently working as a SDTE at FzSports.
I transitioned into a leadership role after being a QA Engineer in 2019 and was tasked with the challenge of industrializing and restructuring the Quality Assurance Team to provide a Cross-Company Service that we could plug into any agile team and certify the quality of the products we build. Our automation efforts have allowed us to triple our capacity and ensure the quality of large products.
My drive is to help teams deliver the best products, focusing on professional excellence and building collaborative work environments.
